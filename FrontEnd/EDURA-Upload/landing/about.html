Our Origin Story

Hack to Crack 2.0 Hackathon

Edura was born out of passion and innovation during the Hack to Crack 2.0 hackathon, where Team Infinity Loop identified a critical challenge in children's education: making learning engaging and effective.

Our mission was to create a solution that transforms learning into an exciting, gamified experience that helps children improve their reading and learning skills.

Key Insights

    1. Limited Awareness of Child’s Performance

  • A significant majority of parents (around 65%) feel they do not clearly know their child’s strong and weak subject areas.

    • 2. High Demand for More Information

  • An overwhelming 87% of respondents want tools or support to better understand their child’s academic strengths and weaknesses.

    • 3. Engagement Challenges

  • Most parents (about 65%) report that their children find classes boring, indicating a need for more engaging or personalized approaches

    • 4. Small Minority Fully Informed and Satisfied

  • Only about a quarter (25%) say they fully understand their child’s performance, and very few parents (8%) report that their kids are consistently happy and engaged in school.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Edura? +
Edura is an innovative learning platform that transforms education into an exciting, gamified experience for children.
How does Edura help children learn? +
Through interactive games and personalized learning paths, Edura makes education fun and engaging, focusing on improving reading and cognitive skills.
Is Edura suitable for all grades? +
Edura offers tailored content for grades from Kindergarten to 5th grade, ensuring age-appropriate and effective learning experiences.
How can parents track their child's progress? +
Parents can access a comprehensive dashboard that provides detailed insights into their child's learning journey, achievements, and areas of improvement.
